2) No display / black screen?

Most common causes are TV input/adapter mismatch.

  • Confirm you‚Äôre on the correct TV input
  • Try a different HDMI port (if using an upscaler)
  • Reseat the AV/HDMI adapter and power cycle the TV

Modern TV picture quality (read this first)

PS2 outputs a low-resolution analogue signal. On modern 4K TVs, the adapter + TV scaling makes the biggest difference. ‚ÄúBad graphics‚Äù is often normal when using cheap HDMI dongles or the wrong settings.

Use a better video method and correct settings. For many TVs, Component (YPbPr) or a quality HDMI upscaler gives the biggest improvement. If you tell us your TV model, we can recommend settings.
