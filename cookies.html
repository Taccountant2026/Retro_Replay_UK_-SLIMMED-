Cookie Policy

Updated January 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help the site remember certain information such as preferences or consent status. Some are essential for the site to function, while others help us improve the user experience through anonymous analytics.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies — required for site functionality, such as saving your cookie consent.
  • Analytics cookies — optional and used only after you give consent. We use Google Analytics (GA4) for aggregated, anonymised statistics.

Managing your cookie preferences

You can manage your cookie settings at any time using the button below. This will re-display our consent banner so you can accept or reject analytics cookies.

You can also clear or block cookies through your browser’s privacy settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Data is anonymised, and IP addresses are truncated to protect your privacy. Analytics only loads after you click “Accept” on the cookie banner.

  • Pages visited and actions taken
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website (e.g., search engine, social link)
  • Approximate geographic region (never precise location)

Third-party cookies

We use PayPal for secure checkout. When you click a PayPal button, PayPal may set its own cookies to support payment functionality. These are controlled by PayPal’s own privacy policy.
