Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

Quick summary: We use cookies to measure traffic and improve the website. We do not sell personal data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work properly, remember your preferences, and (optionally) measure how the site is used.

Cookies we use

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (if used).
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand usage and improve the site (Google Analytics).

Retro Replay UK aims to keep cookies minimal. If we add new tools in future, this policy will be updated.

Your choices

You can accept or reject analytics cookies using our cookie banner. You can also control cookies in your browser settings.

Blocking all cookies may affect some website functionality on some sites. This site should remain usable if analytics are rejected.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure website traffic and understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are visited and how long users spend on the site). This helps us improve content, navigation, and overall customer experience.

What data can be collected (typical):
  • Pages visited and actions taken (e.g. clicks, scrolling behaviour)
  • Approximate location (based on IP region, not your exact address)
  • Device/browser information (e.g. mobile vs desktop)
  • Referral source (e.g. came from search, social, direct)

We do not use Google Analytics to identify you personally. Where possible, we enable privacy-friendly settings.

Google Analytics may set cookies to distinguish users and measure usage. You can opt out by rejecting analytics in our banner and/or using Google’s browser add-on (if available for your browser).

Managing cookies

You can delete cookies and control cookie behaviour in your browser settings. Most browsers let you:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block third-party cookies

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time (for example, if we add new tools or features). Please check this page periodically for updates.
